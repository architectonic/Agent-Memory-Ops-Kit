# Decision Model

Use this model to make project decisions explicit and reviewable.

A decision is a chosen direction with context, reason, consequence, and status.

## Template

```md
# Decision Title

Date:
Status: proposed | accepted | superseded | rejected
Scope:

## Context

What problem or tradeoff caused this decision?

## Options

1. Option one
2. Option two
3. Option three

## Decision

What was chosen?

## Reason

Why was it chosen?

## Consequences

What becomes easier?
What becomes harder?
What must future agents preserve?

## Review Trigger

When should this decision be revisited?
```

## Rules

- Do not record vibes as decisions.
- Do not hide tradeoffs.
- Do not let obsolete decisions silently control future work.
- Link to source artifacts when possible.
