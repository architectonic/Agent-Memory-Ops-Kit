# Decision Review Skill

## Trigger

Use when evaluating a proposal, architecture choice, refactor direction, dependency, or irreversible change.

## Inputs

- Proposed decision.
- Context.
- Alternatives.
- Constraints.
- Evidence.

## Procedure

1. Define the actual problem.
2. Identify evidence that the problem exists.
3. Identify options.
4. Compare consequences.
5. Identify assumptions.
6. Identify what would falsify the preferred option.
7. Recommend the smallest coherent next move.

## Verification

The decision should be traceable to evidence and constraints, not preference alone.

## Failure Modes

- Choosing from vibes.
- Optimizing for elegance without solving the problem.
- Adding complexity before need is proven.
- Ignoring reversibility and risk.
